Carrie's Guide to the World Wide Web

Last updated

I wanted to make an index of a lot of the pages on the Internet that I enjoy, or that have helped me learn about tech, hacking, or anything else. This index is modelled after Jerry's Guide to the World Wide Web, aka the original version of Yahoo :-)

A quick disclaimer: some of these sites are frequently updated and feature the opinions of others. I will try as much as possible to link to individual articles, but sometimes I will not. This doesn't mean that I agree with all opinions expressed on these sites. In fact, sometimes I heavily disagree with the opinions expressed. That does not mean that I think that these people's opinions should be stifled. I have learned a lot from the people I disagree with.
