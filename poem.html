A Secret Poem

Posted May 22, 2024

Here is my hug that I give to you today,
A soft, trembling, forced thing.
I hold you like an unwanted petâ€“
I cradle you like an eggshell that I am taking to the bin.

At night, I always think of you.
I dream that you fall into my arms and it feels like embracing a small sun.
In the dark, we orbit each other, unmarred.

When I wake, your small form is sprawled in a corner.
You stir with me.
You reach for me like a baby, and here I am, repulsed,
Offering you my half-embrace
In exchange for another day of living.
