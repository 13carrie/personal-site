Poem and other things

Posted 14 June, 2024

I hope you are all doing well. I wrote this poem at 3 am the other night:

Secret Rush

a mournful horn on a summer night
the infinite exhale of the fan in the next room
highway hum
window screen vibrations from the sweet sounds outside
